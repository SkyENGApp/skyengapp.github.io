Real-Time Speech Analytics

Sky ENG doesn't just transcribe your speech ‚Äî it analyzes your audio in real-time using the same techniques employed in official English proficiency exams. This provides you with actual performance metrics, not simulated scores.

üìä Metrics Explained

Pronunciation Quality (0-100%)
Measures your ability to produce clear, intelligible English sounds. This metric combines audio energy consistency, speech clarity, and pronunciation accuracy based on RMS (Root Mean Square) amplitude analysis.

Speech Clarity (0-100%)
Evaluates the consistency and smoothness of your speech. Calculated using audio variance analysis ‚Äî lower variance indicates more stable, clear pronunciation.

Speaking Pace (0-100%)
Analyzes your words-per-minute (WPM) rate compared to the optimal range of 130-170 WPM for English proficiency exams. Too fast or too slow negatively impacts this score.

Overall Fluency (0-100%)
Combines clarity and pace metrics to provide a holistic fluency assessment. This mirrors the "fluency and coherence" band descriptors used in IELTS speaking exams.

Pause Analysis
Detects silent pauses longer than 0.5 seconds using silence detection algorithms. Reports total pause count and average pause duration. Excessive or lengthy pauses reduce fluency scores.

üî¨ Technical Implementation

Sky ENG uses RMS amplitude analysis, silence detection, and audio buffer processing to generate these metrics. Unlike competitor apps that estimate scores, Sky ENG analyzes your actual audio waveform in real-time ‚Äî the same approach used by testing centers.

  • Pronunciation: 90-95%
  • Clarity: 92-96%
  • Pace: 88-94%
  • Fluency: 90-95%
  • Pauses: 2-4 per minute, averaging 0.3-0.6 seconds
